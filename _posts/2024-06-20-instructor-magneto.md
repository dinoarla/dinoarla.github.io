Led a technical knowledge transfer session on "MAGNETO: Machine Learning for Ranking System to Prioritize P2TL Target Operation" at PLN Distribution Unit of West Java. Trained operational teams on using the MAGNETO system for data-driven electricity theft detection targeting — covering the ML methodology, system interface, interpretation of ranking outputs, and integration into field operations. This system has since delivered an estimated IDR 334 billion in annual recovered revenue.
